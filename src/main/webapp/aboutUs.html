Galileo Computers is the world's leading manufacturer of customized high-end computer hardware solutions. We source specialized computer parts via reverse auctions and guarantee delivery of all hardware within 14 days. Our clients include over half of the world's Fortune 500 companies as well as numerous governments and universities.

Founded in 1994, we are headquartered in Dublin, Ireland. We started out by serving the Irish and UK markets. In 1999 we expanded into mainland Europe, and since 2003 we have been supplying customers around the world.

Over the years we have developed relationships with a huge number of computer parts suppliers. It is this unparalleled network of suppliers that allows us to quickly piece together be-spoke solutions that are tailored to each individual customer and that are cheaper than any of our competitors.
